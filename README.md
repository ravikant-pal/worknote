**WorkNote** is a free note-taking app that works entirely in your browser — no account, no server, no subscription. Your notes are yours, always.

👉 **[Open WorkNote](https://ravikant-pal.github.io/worknote/)**

---

## What makes WorkNote different?

Most note apps store your data on their servers. WorkNote doesn't. Everything lives on **your device** — and when you share a note, it goes directly to the other person with no middleman.

- ✅ No sign-up, no email, no password
- ✅ Works offline
- ✅ Nobody can read your private notes — not even us
- ✅ Free, forever, no hidden plans
- ✅ Install it like an app on your phone or desktop

## Getting started

### 1. Open the app

Go to **[ravikant-pal.github.io/worknote](https://ravikant-pal.github.io/worknote/)** in any modern browser.

### 2. Set your name

Enter a display name — this is what collaborators will see when you share notes with them. That's all the setup there is.

### 3. Start writing

Click **+ New** to create your first note. Type `/` anywhere in the editor to see everything you can insert — headings, bullet lists, code blocks, images, and more.

---

## Writing notes

WorkNote's editor works like Notion. Click anywhere and start typing, or use the `/` menu to insert different types of content.

**Things you can add to a note:**

- Headings (H1, H2, H3)
- Bullet and numbered lists
- Toggle / collapsible sections
- Code blocks
- Images — just paste or drag them in
- Quotes and callouts

Your notes **save automatically** as you type. No save button needed.

---

## Folders

Keep things tidy by organizing your notes into folders.

- Click **+** next to FOLDERS to create a new folder
- Click a folder to see only its notes
- Click **All Notes** to see everything at once
- Hover a folder and click **···** to rename or delete it

## Sharing notes

Every note has a **share button** in the top right corner.

### Private sharing (default)

1. Click the share icon on a note
2. Click **Generate link**
3. Copy the link and send it to someone — via WhatsApp, email, wherever you like

The link contains everything the other person needs to open and read the note. Nobody else can access it, even if they see the link URL — the decryption key is embedded in the link and never touches any server.

### Public notes

Toggle the note to **Public** if you want it to be discoverable by anyone on the network. Great for publishing guides, announcements, or anything you want the world to see.

### Letting someone edit your note

In the share dialog, paste the collaborator's **public key** (they can find it on their profile page) into the collaborators field. They'll be able to edit the note and their changes will sync to you in real time.

## Installing WorkNote as an app

WorkNote can be installed on your device so it opens like a native app — no browser bar, works offline.

**On desktop (Chrome / Edge):**
Look for the install icon in the address bar and click it.

**On iPhone / iPad:**
Tap the Share button in Safari → **Add to Home Screen**.

**On Android:**
Tap the three-dot menu in Chrome → **Add to Home Screen** or **Install app**.

Once installed, WorkNote works fully offline. Your notes are always available even without an internet connection.

## Your profile

Tap your avatar in the top corner to open your profile. Here you can:

- Change your display name
- Copy your **public key** — share this with collaborators so they can give you edit access to their notes
- Back up your **private key** — this is how WorkNote identifies you. If you lose it you lose access to your synced notes. Keep it somewhere safe like a password manager

---

## Privacy & security

- Your private notes are **end-to-end encrypted**. Nobody — including us — can read them.
- WorkNote has **no servers**. Notes sync through open Nostr relays which store only encrypted data.
- There are **no ads**, no tracking, no analytics.
- Your private key never leaves your device.

---

## Tips

- **Lost a shared note?** Ask the sender to resend the link — the link is everything.
- **Switching devices?** Back up your private key from the profile page and restore it on your new device to keep your identity and synced notes.
- **No internet?** No problem. WorkNote works fully offline and syncs automatically when you reconnect.
